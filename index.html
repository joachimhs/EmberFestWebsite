Learn more about EmberFest 2017

EmberFest is the European Community Ember Conference. If you're looking for updates on the latest and greatest in Ember and Glimmer this is the place to be. EmberFest is also a great opportunity to get in touch with the European Ember Community (and friends from abroad) and hiring Ember talent.

To stay up-to-date, please follow us on Twitter and subscribe to our mailing list.

The Venue

EmberFest 2017 will be held at Villa Elisabeth in the center of Berlin.

Villa Elisabeth is a beautiful venue because of its spacious layout and historical flair. EmberFest will take place in the main event hall.
